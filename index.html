About

A Pioneer in New York State Spirits

Overlooking the shimmering waters of Seneca Lake, Finger Lakes Distilling has been crafting award-winning spirits since 2007 — making it the region's first stand-alone distillery. Founded by Brian McKenzie and Thomas Earl McKenzie, the two combined a background in finance with deep expertise in winemaking, brewing, and traditional distilling to build something the Finger Lakes had never seen before.

As a New York State Farm Distillery, every bottle starts close to home. Locally grown grains and farm-fresh fruits flow through two copper stills — a 350-gallon German-made pot still and a 25-foot continuous still — producing a portfolio of handcrafted whiskeys, vodka, gin, brandy, grappa, and liqueurs that have put this stretch of Seneca Lake on the national spirits map.

What's in the Glass

Award-Winning Spirits Portfolio

From grain-to-glass McKenzie Whiskey to the locally inspired Seneca Drums Gin, every expression at FLD is made with intention — no shortcuts, no extracts, just real ingredients and time-honored technique.

McKenzie Whiskey
Bottled-in-bond bourbon, rye, and Empire Rye — aged in new charred oak and finished in local wine casks
Seneca Drums Gin
Named New York's Best Spirit — a distinctive gin crafted with locally sourced botanicals
Vintner's Vodka
Distilled from Finger Lakes grapes — smooth, elegant, with unmistakable local character
Brandy & Grappa
Pear, apple, and grape brandies made with local fruit — less sweet, more expressive than the national norm
Liqueurs
Fruit-soaked liqueurs made with real, whole ingredients — never extracts or artificial flavors
Glen Thunder
Corn whiskey with a bold, distinctly New York character — unaged and unapologetic
Visit

More Than a Tasting Room

The Tippling House is your gateway to the full Finger Lakes Distilling experience — flights, cocktails, light bites, and sweeping lake views, all under one roof. But there's plenty more to explore.

Tasting Flights & Cocktails

Walk up any day, no reservation needed for groups under 12. Sip a flight or settle in for a craft cocktail while watching production happen right through the glass.

Distillery Tours

Get an up-close look at the two copper stills, the production process, and the history behind one of New York's most celebrated craft spirits operations.

Make Your Own Gin

A hands-on workshop for groups of 1–8. Blend your own botanicals, distill your personal recipe, and walk away with a custom bottle of gin you made yourself.

Thief & Mallet Club

The ultimate insider experience — join the club to make your own whiskey from grain to bottle, with barrel selection, blending sessions, and exclusive member events.
